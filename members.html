Immersive Empathic Interface Lab

Dr. Sungchul Jung
Assistant Professor, Lab Supervisor

I am an Assistant Professor of Computer Game Design and Development at Kennesaw State University. I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Central Florida in 2018. I was a postdoctoral fellow researcher at the Human Interface Technology Lab (HIT Lab) in New Zealand, from 2018 to 2021. My research interests lie in the field of eXtended Reality (VR/MR/AR) and data-driven Empathic Computing based Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) perspective. Based on the findings from my research, I am leveraging the fully personalized virtual reality experience for social relationships, education, learning and collaboration, entertainment, simulation, game design, and healthcare. I am actively working on academic service as a reviewer, program committee, and editor at prestigious venues such as IEEE VR, ACM VRST, IEEE ISMAR, ACM CHI, and Frontiers.
